History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup for software engineers. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the world’s largest gatherings for tech professionals and startups.

Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of technology for good. TechCon connects innovators, developers, and startups to build solutions that make a positive impact.
